QUEEN ALBUMS RANKER

This website is all about ranking Queen Albums (Opinion Based)

1. Made In Heaven

Made In Heaven was the last album Queen Realeased, it was realeased on the 6th of November ,1995, 5 Years after Freddie's Death.

2. A Kind Of Magic

After Queen took a break from the recording studio between 1985-1985, they recorded their 12th Album, A Kind Of Magic, sadly, this was the last toured album after Freddie was diagnosed with AIDS the following year

3. Sheer Heart Attack

Sheer Heart Attack was the 3rd studio album released by queen, it was released in 1974 and featured one of queens greatest hits, killer queen.

4. A Night At The Opera

After queen almost went bankrupt, they recorded and released their most ambitious album at the time, it was their 5th studio album released in 1975 and went platinum extremely fast. It features one of the world's most popular songs Bohemian Rhapsody.

5. Innuendo

Innuendo is queen's last album released before freddie's death in 1991.
